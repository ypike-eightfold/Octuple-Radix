**WCAG AA 2.2 Compliant** - All components have been audited and enhanced for maximum accessibility:

- **Enhanced Contrast**: Form controls (Switch, Checkbox, Radio) now meet 4.5:1 contrast minimum
- **Visible Focus Indicators**: Consistent indigo focus rings on all interactive elements

- **Screen Reader Ready**: Proper ARIA labels, roles, and state announcements
- **Color Independence**: Information conveyed through more than just color
